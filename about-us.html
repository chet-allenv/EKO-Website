Activities

At EKO, we offer a wide range of activities designed to promote Greek culture. From on campus to off, we are excited to share our heritage with the community. From cultural events like traditional Greek dancing and food tastings, to educational workshops about Greek history, language, and traditions, and to social gatherings and community service projects, there is something for everyone. Our activities are aimed at promoting Greek heritage, fostering a sense of community, and providing opportunities for personal and professional growth.
